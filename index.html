About us

Kelnir Projects was founded by Kuvern Naidoo in June 2000.

Kuvern has 24 years experience at companies such as Johannesburg water, Sasol, AECI and Omnia, filling various technical, marketing and leadership roles. His main industry focus throughout his career has been water management, chemical sales and engineering.

His education background comprises of the following:

Degree in Chemical Engineering
Post Graduate Diploma in Business Management
Masters in Business Administration

Why we do it

To deliver sustainable solutions to out partners with the intentof extending longevity of resources as such as food and water for the sake of our children.

How we do it?

1.    Relentless research and innovation in water management, waste recycling and environmental technologies
2.   We share the knowledge we acquire, remaining cogniscent of the newest research and trends
3.   We strive to ensure our clients maintain and improve profitability while achieving their environmental goals.

What we do?

We focus on delivering our products and services through our three divisions

1.    Consulting Solutions through knowledge
2.   Resource Management & Engineering
3.   Solutions through research, technology and innovation
